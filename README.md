# Friction – A Necessary Evil

## Description

Friction is a force that opposes motion between two surfaces in contact. While friction causes energy loss and wear, it is also essential for many everyday activities. This project demonstrates the importance of friction through educational content, interactive simulations, and quizzes.

## Problem Statement

Many students understand friction only as a force that resists motion. However, they often fail to recognize that friction is both beneficial and harmful in different situations. Understanding this balance is important in physics and engineering.

## Solution

This project provides an interactive platform that explains the concept of friction, highlights its positive and negative impacts, and allows users to explore friction levels through simulations and quizzes.

## Features

* Introduction to friction
* Positive effects of friction
* Negative effects of friction
* Interactive friction simulator
* Educational quiz section
